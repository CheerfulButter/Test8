posted an hour ago by HS Office

Verse of the Day: So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand. Isaiah 41:10

Today is an A day.

New Announcements: 

Juniors:  Bible study will meet  on Wednesday, Jan. 18, at 5:30 at the Cagwin's.  Dinner and discussion as usual.  Come and start the new semester with us!

Students: The Science National Honor Society will be meeting on Wednesday in S215 after class meetings.  Newbies are welcome.  Members should remember to pay their NT$200 membership fee.  We will discuss MS Science Fair and if time permits watch a video.

Animal Club members - please read your email about a trip this Saturday and respond by the end of Wednesday.
_______________________________________

ALL: There is an opportunity for us to share in a ministry as a community. We are collecting items from hotels such as toothbrushes, shampoo, soap, body wash and other small items (candy,gum), for a local church that ministers to the homeless.  The Junior class will help gather these supplies, package them and get them delivered.  Our goal is to get these to the church to be given out before Chinese New Year (Sunday, January 22nd). There is a box inside the office where these items can be dropped off. Thank you. (Bev. McCrary)
 
Parents: Please check out the HS In Touch for January.

ALL: Is your home filled with items you no longer use?  Do you have an urge to downsize?  Fear not!  The Senior Flea Market is Saturday, March 11.  For only 600NT, you can rent a table to sell all your slightly used items.  Tables go on sale on Wednesday, Feb. 8th at 7:30 am. near the student store. 
*limit of 3 tables per person
*tables must be reserved in person on Wed. Feb. 8th  (No advanced reservations or reservations through the HS/EMS office or Security Desk)
*table locations can NOT be requested or reserved
The seniors will receive donations after Chinese New Year break -- more information to follow.  (jm)
